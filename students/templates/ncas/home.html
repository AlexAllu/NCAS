Ragging Free
NCAS is a 100% ragging-free campus. Anti-Ragging Cell In order to ensure zero-ragging at NCAS, the institution has constituted an Anti-Ragging Cell strictly in accordance with the orders of the Hon. Supreme Court as well as the University Grants Commission. The Committee consists of members from the Teaching and the Administrative staff, PTA and the student community. The objective of the cell is to eliminate ragging in all its forms from the institution by prohibiting it under these Regulations, preventing its occurrence and punishing those who indulge in ragging as provided for in these Regulations and the relevant law in force.
